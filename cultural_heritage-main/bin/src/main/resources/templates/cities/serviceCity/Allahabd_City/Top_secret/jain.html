Tirthankar Rishabhdev Diksha Tirth

No of Temples: 3+1 (Kirtistambh/Glory pillar), Pahaad/Mountain: An artificial hillock 127 steps. Historical Importance: As per Jain Scriptures, this is the place where the Jungle namely Siddharth exited before in which Lord Vrushabhnath accepted Jain Diksha attained omniscience. A beautiful construction is done under motivation of Aaryika Gyanmatiji in February 2001. The ordination (Dikasha) , Tapowan, Samavsaran, Kailash mountain, cave Temple, Glory pillar are worth to see and bow. A 14 feet tall sitting posture (Padmasan) Idol, Trikal Choubisin 72 Temples are worth to see. Annual Fair: Lord Vrushabdev Diksha Kalyanak- Chaitra Vadi 9 to 11, annual fair. Lord Vrushabdev Nirwan Kalyanak- Magh K.14. Tirthkshetra established day- Magh Shukl 13.
