Culture & Heritage

Indian culture and heritage are a rich and diverse tapestry, shaped by the country's history, geography, and religious diversity. Here are some key aspects. Spiritual Heritage: India is the birthplace of several religions, including Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism. These faiths have influenced Indian thought, art, and architecture.Traditional Arts: Classical music (Carnatic and Hindustani), dance forms (Bharatanatyam, Kathak, etc.), and theater (Kathakali, etc.) are integral to Indian culture.
