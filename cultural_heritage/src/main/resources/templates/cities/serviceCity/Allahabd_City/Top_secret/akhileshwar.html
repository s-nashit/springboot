AKhileshwar Temple, Prayagraj

Akhileshwar Temple built from Rajasthani sandstone is situated at Rasulabad Ghat in Teliarganj. The structure of this small sized temple has been done very finely, whose beauty is the main center of its attraction. A huge crowd of people is seen here in the evening.

Shree Akhileshwar Mahadev complex is spread over an area of about 500 sq. feet in Prayagraj near Rasulabad Ghat road under Chinmaya Mission. The foundation stones were laid on 30th October, 2004 by Parampoojya Swami Tejomayanandaji and Poojya Swami Subodhanandaji of Chinmaya Mission. Pink sand stones from Rajasthan are being carved and fixed to give shape to Shree Akhileshwar Mahadev Dhyan Mandapam over the Foundation floor. The foundation floor is designed to accommodate a Satsang Bhavan having a capacity of about 300 persons and all the essential services for Shree Akhileshwar Mahadev.

As far the as architectural beauty of this place is concerned, the architecture of this temple is as per specifications in the scriptures of Hindus. Having the beautiful pink stone and the white marble as the part of the construction, make this place look amazingly beautiful. Inside the temple, there is an idol of Lord Shiva made with the black marble. This is the place where devotees worship Lord Shiva. Therefore, you must visit this place. Here, in addition to getting the beautiful view of this temple, you may also feel some real Shiv-Bhakti.

Timings: 6 AM - 12 PM, 4 PM - 9 PM
